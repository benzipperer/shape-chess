Game Rules

Objective: First player to reach 4 points wins.

Shapes: A shape is a group of same-colored stones connected orthogonally or diagonally. A shape is symmetric if it can be reflected along a line.

Moves:

  • Drop: Place your stone on any empty point.
  • Jump: Move one of your stones to any empty point.
  • Push: Push an opponent's stone to an adjacent empty point and place your stone where it was.

Scoring: After your turn, if you have symmetric shapes of 6+ stones, they are removed and you score (n-5) points per shape. You then take another turn!
